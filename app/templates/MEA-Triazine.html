Frequently Asked Questions About MEA Triazine

What is MEA Triazine? ▾

MEA Triazine is a triazine-based compound widely used as a hydrogen sulfide (H₂S) scavenger in oil and gas industries.

What industries use MEA Triazine?▾

MEA Triazine is used in oilfields, refineries, wastewater treatment, and paper industries for efficient H₂S removal.

Do you export MEA Triazine?▾

Yes, Vasudev Chemo Pharma exports MEA Triazine globally, with a focus on Gulf countries and international oil & gas sectors.

Is MEA Triazine suitable for crude oil treatment?▾

Yes, MEA Triazine effectively removes hydrogen sulfide from crude oil and helps prevent corrosion and odor issues.

Is MEA Triazine formaldehyde-free?▾

Yes, MEA Triazine is formaldehyde-free, making it safer for both handling and the environment.

What is the typical dosage of MEA Triazine?▾

The dosage depends on H₂S concentration and system conditions. Typically, 1 mole of triazine reacts with 2 moles of H₂S.

What is the shelf life of MEA Triazine?▾

MEA Triazine has a typical shelf life of 12 months when stored in tightly sealed containers away from direct sunlight.

Is MEA Triazine water soluble?▾

Yes, MEA Triazine is highly water soluble, making it ideal for aqueous H₂S scavenging systems.

How does MEA Triazine work?▾

MEA Triazine reacts chemically with hydrogen sulfide to form stable, non-volatile by-products that prevent H₂S release.

Can MEA Triazine be used in gas sweetening?▾

Yes, it is frequently used for gas sweetening applications to remove H₂S from natural gas streams.

What is the appearance of MEA Triazine?▾

It appears as a clear to pale yellow liquid with a mild amine-like odor.

What are the storage conditions for MEA Triazine?▾

Store in cool, dry, and well-ventilated areas in HDPE or stainless-steel containers.

Can MEA Triazine be mixed with other chemicals?▾

It should not be mixed with oxidizing or acidic agents as it may cause unwanted reactions.

Is MEA Triazine environmentally safe?▾

Yes, it produces non-toxic, biodegradable by-products, making it an environmentally friendly scavenger.
